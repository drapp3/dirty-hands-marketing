The Difference

Why we don't use WordPress.

Most agencies use templates because templates are cheap and fast to build. They are also slow, bloated, and a nightmare to maintain. We took the harder path on purpose.

A template site loads up to 3 megabytes of code your business will never use. Every plugin adds attack surface for hackers. Every theme update risks breaking the site. The end result: a site that loads in 5 to 8 seconds, ranks poorly, and costs you more in maintenance over 2 years than the original build did.

Hand-coded sites carry only the code your business actually needs. They load in under a second. They never need plugin updates because they have no plugins. The cost of doing it right once is lower than the cost of fighting a template forever.

Questions

Common questions.

How long does a website build take?
Most builds go live in 2 to 3 weeks. The exact timeline depends on how fast you can review designs and provide content. We move as fast as you do.
Do I own the website when it's built?
Yes. The code is yours. The domain is yours. The hosting account is in your name. If you ever leave us, you walk away with everything. No hostage situations.
Can I update the site myself?
Most updates we handle for you as part of the monthly retainer. For larger content changes, we can either teach you the basics or just handle them. Whichever you prefer.
What if I already have a website?
We offer free audits of existing sites. We will show you what's working, what's broken, and what it would take to either fix the current site or rebuild from scratch. No obligation.
Why is hand-coded better than WordPress for my business?
Speed, security, and long-term cost. Your customers care about a site that loads fast. Google ranks fast sites higher. And you don't want to spend the next 5 years paying someone to manage plugin updates and theme conflicts.
