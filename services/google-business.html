The Difference

Why your Google profile matters more than your website.

Most contractors spend 90% of their marketing budget on the website and ignore the listing that drives 70% of their leads. We see this every week.

When someone searches "concrete contractor near me" on their phone, Google shows three results before the website list. Those three results are Google Business Profiles. If you're not in those three, most searchers never scroll far enough to see your website.

A great GBP with strong reviews and complete information beats a great website with a weak profile every time. Customers see your reviews, your photos, your hours, your services, and your call button before they ever visit your site. Most of them call directly from the listing without clicking through.

Questions

Common questions.

I already have a Google Business Profile. Why do I need help?
Most contractor profiles are 30% complete. Wrong categories, old photos, no service areas, no posts in 6 months. We clean up what's there and turn it into a profile that ranks and wins clicks.
How long until I show up in the local 3-pack?
First visibility in 30 days for less competitive searches. The top 3 in your main service category usually takes 3 to 6 months depending on how strong the competition is in your city.
What if my profile was suspended or removed?
We've handled suspensions before. The process to reinstate depends on why it happened. We start with an audit to figure out the cause and the right path back.
Do you handle reviews?
We don't fake reviews. We help you build a system to ask real customers at the right time, and we respond to every review you get (positive and negative) in your voice. Anyone offering review services beyond that is selling you risk.
Can you manage profiles for multiple locations?
Yes. Multi-location businesses need a slightly different approach. Each location gets its own profile, optimized for its specific service area. Pricing scales by location.
