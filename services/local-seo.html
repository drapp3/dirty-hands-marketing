The Difference

Why local SEO beats paid ads.

Ads stop the moment you stop paying. Rankings compound. Most contractors learn this the hard way after burning $20,000 on Google Ads with nothing to show for it.

Paid ads cost between $40 and $150 per click in the trades. Most clicks never become calls. The ones that do cost more than the job is worth on smaller projects. And the moment your monthly budget runs out, the leads stop.

Local SEO works the opposite way. You invest once. Rankings build over 3 to 6 months. Once you're ranking, the calls keep coming whether you spend more this month or not. By month 12, your cost per lead is a fraction of what ads would charge.

Questions

Common questions.

How long until I see SEO results?
First small movements in 30 to 60 days. Real ranking gains by month 3. Compounding traffic by month 6. Anyone promising faster than that is either lying or running spammy tactics that get penalized later.
Will I rank for "roofer near me"?
Eventually, in your specific city, yes. The exact phrase depends on your trade and competition. We focus on the searches that actually convert, not just the ones with the highest volume.
Do I need both SEO and Google Ads?
Not at first. SEO compounds for free. Ads are a tax you pay every month. Once SEO is ranking and producing leads, you can layer ads on top to capture even more market. Most of our clients never need ads.
What if I work in multiple cities?
We build location pages for each city you serve. Each one is optimized for that specific market. This is how you rank in Mooresville and Concord and Hickory at the same time without stuffing keywords into a single page.
Can you guarantee a number 1 ranking?
No. Anyone who guarantees a specific ranking is lying. Google's algorithm changes constantly. We can guarantee the work, the process, and the foundation. Rankings are the output of doing the work right over time.
