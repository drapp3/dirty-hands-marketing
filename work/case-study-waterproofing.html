The problem

An outdated website that wasn't generating leads or ranking for anything that mattered.

The client is a waterproofing and restoration company serving the greater Charlotte area. Foundation repair, basement waterproofing, crawl space encapsulation, water damage restoration. The kind of work where customers are actively searching for help and calling whoever shows up first.

The existing website was slow, wasn't optimized for local search, and wasn't built to convert visitors into calls. Competitors with better websites were showing up higher in search results and getting the leads that should've been going to the company.

>

Slow load times killing both rankings and visitor patience. Google penalizes sites that take more than 3 seconds.

>

No local SEO foundation. Missing schema markup, weak meta descriptions, no keyword strategy for service + location searches.

>

No clear conversion path. Visitors landed on the site but had no obvious next step to request service or call.

>

Not mobile-friendly enough. Most people searching for emergency waterproofing or foundation issues are on their phones.

What we built

A complete rebuild focused on speed, search, and getting the phone to ring.

We rebuilt the site from scratch as a hand-coded static site. Every decision was about two things: rank higher in local search and make it as easy as possible for someone to call.

01 Full rebuild, zero bloat

Hand-coded HTML and CSS. No WordPress, no page builders, no plugins. The result is a site that loads in under 1 second on any device and any connection.

02 Dedicated service pages

Individual pages for basement waterproofing, foundation repair, crawl space encapsulation, and water damage restoration. Each one targets specific local search terms.

03 Local SEO from day one

JSON-LD schema markup, optimized meta descriptions, keyword-focused headings. Structured to rank for "waterproofing contractor near me" and similar searches across the Charlotte metro.

04 Mobile-first design

Built for phones first. Large tap targets, sticky call button, phone number always one tap away. 70%+ of local service searches happen on mobile.

05 Conversion-focused layout

Phone number in the header, contact forms on every service page, strong calls-to-action throughout. No ambiguity about what to do next.

06 Hosted on Vercel

Deployed on Vercel's edge network. Automatic SSL, global CDN, zero downtime. Enterprise-grade infrastructure at no hosting cost.
