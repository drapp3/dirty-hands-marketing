The problem

No website. No Google presence. Losing jobs to competitors who had both.

Old North Concrete is a concrete contractor working across North Carolina. Good work, good reputation with past clients, steady referrals. But zero web presence.

When someone searched "concrete contractor near me," Old North didn't show up. Their competitors did. Every day that meant potential customers calling someone else.

>

No website at all. Customers couldn't verify they were a real, active business.

>

No Google Business Profile. Invisible in Google Maps and local search results.

>

Relying entirely on word-of-mouth. No way to capture inbound leads from search.

>

Competitors with decent websites were getting the calls that should've been theirs.

What we built

A fast, conversion-focused site built to rank from day one.

We built a hand-coded static website designed around one goal: get people to call. Every page, every section, every design decision ties back to that.

01 Custom static site

Hand-coded HTML and CSS. No WordPress, no page builders, no bloat. Loads in under 1 second on any device.

02 Mobile-first design

Built for phones first since that's where most local searches happen. Clean layout, big tap targets, phone number always visible.

03 Local SEO foundation

Keyword-optimized headings, meta descriptions, and JSON-LD schema markup. Structured to rank for "concrete contractor" + location searches.

04 Google Business Profile

Set up and optimized their GBP listing. Photos, service categories, service areas, and a review strategy to build credibility over time.

05 Clear calls-to-action

Phone number in the header, contact forms on key pages, and a sticky mobile call button. No confusion about what to do next.

06 Deployed on Vercel

Hosted on Vercel's edge network for fast global delivery, automatic SSL, and zero downtime. Free hosting with professional-grade infrastructure.
